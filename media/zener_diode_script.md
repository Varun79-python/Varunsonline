# Zener Diode Explainer (30-40 sec)

## Visual: Show a regular diode vs zener diode symbol

**Narrator:** A Zener diode is like a regular diode but with a superpower.

## Visual: Current flowing forward (normal)

**Narrator:** In forward bias, it works normally — current flows.

## Visual: Reverse bias with breakdown animation, voltage label "Zener Voltage"

**Narrator:** But in reverse bias, instead of blocking current, it breaks down at a precise voltage — called the Zener voltage — and conducts safely.

## Visual: Voltage regulator circuit (diode + resistor, output steady)

**Narrator:** This lets it regulate voltage. No matter the input, the output stays constant at the Zener voltage.

## Visual: Real-world uses — phone charger, power supply

**Narrator:** You'll find them in power supplies, voltage regulators, and protection circuits.

**Narrator:** Simple, precise, and reliable.
